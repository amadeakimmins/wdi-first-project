Colour Match

Instructions

Match the colour of the word shown but not the name of the word.


For example, if the word 'RED' is printed in a orange colour then you click the 'ORANGE' button NOT the red button.


Each round is timed and the timer will decrease after every round so you've got to be quick!


There will be 6 different levels of increasing difficulty. They will be explained at the beginning of each level.
